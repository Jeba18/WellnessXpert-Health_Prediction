About HealthPredictor

HealthPredictor is an AI-powered health risk assessment platform designed to help users assess the risk of chronic diseases such as:

  • Heart Disease
  • Diabetes
  • Obesity
  • Asthma

Our system leverages medical data and machine learning algorithms to provide instant risk predictions and lifestyle recommendations.

With a simple and user-friendly interface, HealthPredictor empowers individuals to take charge of their health in a proactive way.
