About HealthPredictor

HealthPredictor is an AI-powered health risk assessment platform that empowers individuals to take control of their well-being.

It helps users assess the risk of chronic diseases such as:

  • Heart Disease
  • Diabetes
  • Obesity
  • Asthma

Our system uses real-time medical data and advanced machine learning to provide:

  • Instant risk predictions
  • Personalized lifestyle and health tips
  • Simple, user-friendly interfaces for everyone
